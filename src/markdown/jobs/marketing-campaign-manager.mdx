## Marketing Campaign Manager

<About />

Ory is the largest open-source authentication and authorization (login,
registration, MFA, permissions, OAuth2,...) ecosystem and community in the world. We
develop open-source software and use it to build a scalable, easy-to-use, and
secure cloud platform. You will provide organizational support to Ory leadership
to ensure smooth operations and communications. Together with the CEO, you will
provide a core leadership function that collaborates with UX design,
engineering, finance, marketing, and data analytics.

### Overview

- **Position Type:** Full Time
- **Location**: Munich, Germany
- **Components**: Google Suite, Slack, Github

### What You'll Do

- Conceptualize and execute marketing campaigns to expand Ory’s reach among prospects and customers across multiple channels
- Create and manage a Digital Marketing campaign calendar
- Assist team workflow and communicate across different teams
- Collaborate with external Stakeholder when necessary
- Research competitor strategies and industry standards in brand and performance campaigns
- Benchmark innovative and flexible content creation and campaign management workflows
- Make creative, engaging, and impactful marketing content in collaboration with team members
- Grow and shape Ory’s presence as global brand with open source roots
- Explore new channels for reaching relevant audiences for Ory Cloud


### We’re looking for someone who has

- 5+ years experience in digital and performance marketing
- Strong writing skills and ability to explain technical concepts to a wide audience with varying levels of technical expertise
- Experience in creative development and production and collaboration with external stakeholders 
- Advanced understanding of industry standards and emerging trends in digital marketing
- Ability to self-manage and collaborate across teams as well as reporting
- Generating marketing insights and willingness to try out creative new ideas and iterate on them 
- Fluency in written and oral English
