### Overview

- **Position type:** Full time
- **Location**: Munich, Germany / Remote (Timezone CET ± 3H)
- **Components**: Ory Cloud Console, Ory Open Source

### What You'll Do

- Develop the sales pipeline from prospecting to meeting customers to closing
  sales.
- Become an expert on our brand, market, the Ory Cloud, and Ory Open Source
  products.
- Help set the tone for future hires and develop the sales and solutions team.

### We’re looking for someone who has

- Successful experience in a new business generation/inside sales role, or
  equivalent experience.
- A Bachelor’s Degree in an analytical or technical subject field or equivalent
  experience.
- Strong attention to detail, communication, and writing skills. Fluent written
  and spoken German and English skills, any other languages are a plus.
- Experience with sales technology tools like Salesforce, Zapier, Mautic, or
  others.
